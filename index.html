    Projects

  • JoyStick

    • SWC National Third Prize Project. Joystick is a two-way screen projection control software for heterogeneous devices, which aims to realize the interconnection, intercommunication and mutual control of near-field multi-devices. I was responsible for the development of the PC side, and developed functions such as multi-screen collaboration, device discovery, and remote control, realizing near-field communication and control, mutual delivery control between PC and mobile phones, and simulation of gamepads.
